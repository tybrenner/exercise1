My Goals For This Class

I want to gain the knowledge and skills needed for a career relating to coding. I am taking the Web Foundations series to start out, and I plan on taking Javascript Immerssion class if it all works out.

Concerns

My main concern is not knowing if I can commit to coding long enough to keep up with others or new languages. However, this class always leaves me eager to work on assignments and to keep learning, so it does not seem like it will be an issue.

Another concern I have has to do with my chances of getting a job after going through this course and the Javascript Immersion. I am concerned about this because I have not finished college and I only have a part time job, so I am wondering if my job experience or education play as big of a role as the actual skills I gain from PCS.

I have less than average knowledge and a large interest in anything computer related, so the last concern I have with this class is all the different languages I may or may not have to learn. After I take the Javascript Immersion class, I have no idea where to go from there, whether it be to continue my education or to look for a career after the Javascript Immersion class.
